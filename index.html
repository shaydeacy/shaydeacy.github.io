About

Web Developer, based in Cork , Ireland.

I use web technologies to provide a simple, low cost service to start-ups and small businesses, who are looking for their first web presence, or to improve their current one.

I specialize in html, css and javascript, and can build everything web, from simple websites to more complex single page web applications.

All web pages are implemented using Responsive Web Design(RWD), so the page will look just as beautiful on your phone or tablet, as it looks on your desktop computer or laptop.
